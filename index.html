These data are brought to you by the TESS mission. This website was made by Megan Bedell, Ben Montet, and Adina Feinstein.

You can download the data from MAST in a variety of ways. What's plotted here is the median-divided PDCSAP lightcurve for random 2-minute-cadence targets. Check out the lightkurve python package for many great tools for more in-depth interactive lightcurve generation and exploration.
